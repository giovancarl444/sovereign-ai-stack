The two walls everyone hits on OCI

  • “Out of host capacity” on every A1.Flex launch — the cheap ARM shape is massively over-subscribed.
  • 429 Too Many Requests — because the OCI CLI binary storm-retries internally and burns your rate limit before you get a real answer.

This repo is the working fix. It calls the OCI SDK directly (instant, no storm), discovers the ADs that actually belong to your tenancy, and tells you whether to wait (capacity) or fix (format bug).

  --image      ocid1.image.oc1... \
  --subnet     ocid1.subnet.oc1... \
  --ssh-key    ~/.ssh/id_ed25519.pub \
